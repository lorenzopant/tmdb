## `AccountMutationResponse`

Response returned by [`add_favorite()`](/docs/api-reference/account/add-favorite) and [`add_to_watchlist()`](/docs/api-reference/account/add-to-watchlist) on success (HTTP 2xx).
Errors (4xx/5xx) are always thrown as `TMDBError` and never appear here.

## `AccountMovieItem`

A movie entry in an account's favorites or watchlist list. Extends [`MovieResultItem`](/docs/types/search#movieresultitem) with `adult` always present.

<AutoTypeTable path="account.ts" name="AccountMovieItem" />

## `AccountTVItem`

A TV show entry in an account's favorites or watchlist list. Equivalent to [`TVSeriesResultItem`](/docs/types/search#tvseriesresultitem).

## `AccountRatedMovieItem`

A movie from the rated movies list. Extends [`AccountMovieItem`](/docs/types/account#accountmovieitem) with a user `rating` field.
