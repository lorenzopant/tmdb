## 🚀 1.16.0 - 23 March 2026

- ✨ New ✨ [People Lists API](/docs/api-reference/people-lists) — get a paginated list of people ordered by TMDB popularity
- ✨ New ✨ [Reviews API](/docs/api-reference/reviews) — fetch full details of a single TMDB review by its review ID

## 🚀 1.15.1 - 21 March 2026

- 🧪 Improved test coverage
- 📦 Attempt to reduce npm package size

## 🚀 1.15.0 - 21 March 2026

- [Trending API](/docs/api-reference/trending) — get trending movies, TV shows, and people for a given time window (`"day"` or `"week"`)
- AI SKILL.md file for better ai development of this package.

## 🚀 1.14.1 - 20 March 2026

- [`search.tv_series()`](/docs/api-reference/search/tv-series) — search for TV shows by their original, translated, and also known as names
- [`search.multi()`](/docs/api-reference/search/multi) — search across movies, TV shows, and people in a single request, with results discriminated by `media_type`

## 🚀 1.14.0 - 20 March 2026

- [Logging support](/docs/getting-started/options/logging) — enable the built-in console logger with `logger: true` or pass a custom [`TMDBLoggerFn`](/docs/types/logger#tmdbloggerfn) for full control over request, response, and error logging
- [TVSeasons API](/docs/api-reference/tv-seasons) — access detailed information about TV show seasons, including episodes, air dates, and more
